At the start of my sophomore year, I got a job as an expo worker at the Cascade Roadhouse, a local restaurant. I made salads, appetizers, desserts, and would run food to customers.
After nearly a year and half of working at the restaurant, I asked the head chef to train to be a line cook. I now help on the line when needed.
My first job was refereeing youth soccer games. After a couple years, I was consistently the head referee. I continued reffing until the end of my Junior year.
After a year of working at the restaurant, I was promoted to Lead Expo. I am in charge of a group of two to four people everynight ensuring quick times and quality service.
When the restaurant shut down due to COVID, I promptly got a job at Meijer, a grocery store. It was fulfilling knowing that I was helping the community in some sense by working during the pandemic while others couldn't. I continue working at Meijer on my days off from the restaurant.

Head Chef Emilio Rodriguez
